Summary: Initial values vs. Growth in 20 years

The purpose of this project was to analyze the evolution of productivity of Mexican firms from 1993-2013. To accomplish this analysis, we used Mexican Economic Census Data for the mentioned periods and estimated productivity measuares at the firm level.

Productivity variables are analyzed at the Municipality level. We try to establish the existence of "convergence" across Municipalities in Mexico. If convergence is achieved, then Municipalities with the lowest initial values (in 1993) of a given variable are the ones that grew the most during the period (i.e. by 2013, they "catch-up" with the rest).

The results are shown in scatters created with Matplotlib. In most cases, we find the existence of convergence of productivity, as shown in scatters with negative relations. We also perform regression analysis for each exercise, using Numpy's Polyfit.
